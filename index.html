Complex Systems and Big Data

I have also published in the area of Complex Systems. We looked into human mobility patterns, researching the scaling of the length of trips in cities, and comparing our results with conventional survey data to those obtained using Big Data. Our goal was to determine whether human mobility is "super-diffusive" within cities -- that is, do people take more long trips than we might normally expect, especially for fossil-fuel modes? This could have large implications for energy consumption and urban planning. See my project here.
