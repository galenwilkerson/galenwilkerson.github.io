Complex Systems and Big Data

However, I have also published in the area of Complex Systems of Human Mobility, researching the scaling of human trip-lengths in cities, and comparing our results using conventional data to those obtained using Big Data. Our goals was to determine whether human mobility is "super-diffusive" within cities. That is, do people take more long trips than we might normally expect? This could have large implications for energy consumption and urban planning. See my project here.
